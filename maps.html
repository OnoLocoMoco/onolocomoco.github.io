Makena Landing

Makena Landing, Maui, HI

This map of a popular SCUBA diving site on Maui was created using LiDAR data and aerial imagery in ArcGIS Pro. A gentle blue gradient indicates increasing depth as you get further from shore and blue contour lines with labels give depth measurements across the site. 

A small rock known as "Marty's Reef" is visible in the upper left near the compass. 

Ulua Beach

Ulua Beach, Maui, HI

Another very popular SCUBA and snorkeling site. This image, also created with LiDAR and aerial imagery, shows the two distinct rocky reefs located at this south Maui beach park. Due to the shallow beach entry and reefs immediately off-shore, this is one of Maui's most underwater destinations. 

CyRide coverage in Ames, Iowa. 

CyRide Coverage in Ames

We are illustrate the coverage of CyRide routes in Ames with a buffer around the routes. Areas outside of that buffer might be considered "under-served" by CyRide based on the distance residents must walk to reach a route or bus stop. 

We are able to examine the demographics of the census blocks which fall within those possibly-underserved areas. Here we see that neighborhoods in Ames with greater renter-occupied housing are fairly well-served by CyRide.
